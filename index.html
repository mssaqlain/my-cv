SajaoAI
AI Interior Designer · Personal Project

Upload a photo of any room. Pick a style. Get an AI-redesigned version in seconds.
SajaoAI is a cross-platform mobile app (Flutter, Android + iOS) that lets anyone reimagine their room in a new interior design style — Modern, Minimalist, Industrial, Bohemian, and more — without hiring a designer. Built end-to-end solo: product idea, UI/UX design, mobile app, backend, and the AI image-generation pipeline behind it.

💡 The Idea

Interior design consultations are expensive and slow, and most people just want a quick, visual "what if" before committing to repainting a wall or buying new furniture. SajaoAI turns that "what if" into a 15-second AI-generated preview, straight from a phone camera.

⚙️ How It Works
  • Upload — take a photo of a room, or pick one from the gallery.
  • Choose Style — select a room type (bedroom, living room, kitchen, …) and one of 10 design styles.
  • Customize — optionally fine-tune color palette, budget level, furniture density, lighting, and mood.
  • AI Generates — a Stable Diffusion image-to-image model redesigns the room while preserving its structure, walls, windows, and camera angle.
  • Compare & Share — a draggable before/after slider shows the transformation; save both photos to the gallery or share them directly.
✨ Key Features
  • Email, guest (anonymous), and social sign-in — try the app instantly with zero friction, or create an account to save projects.
  • AI room redesign grounded in the user's own photo, not a generic stock render.
  • 10 design styles × 9 room types, plus optional color/budget/furniture/lighting/mood preferences that feed into the AI prompt.
  • Before/after compare slider — an interactive, draggable reveal between the original and generated photo.
  • Download & Share — both photos save to the gallery together, or share to any app via the native share sheet.
  • Credit-based usage, enforced atomically server-side (row-locked Postgres functions) so it can't be bypassed from the client.
  • Dark, luxury-themed UI, designed as an interactive prototype before a single line of app code was written.
